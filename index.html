How to Calculate Percentage of Marks

The percentage formula is universally used in education systems worldwide:

Percentage = (Obtained Marks ÷ Total Marks) × 100

Practical Examples

  • If you score 75 out of 100: (75/100)×100 = 75%
  • If you score 340 out of 500: (340/500)×100 = 68%
