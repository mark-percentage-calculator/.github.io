How to Calculate Marks Percentage

Calculating your marks percentage is a simple process that helps you understand your academic performance. The formula used is:

(Obtained Marks ÷ Total Marks) × 100 = Percentage

For example, if you scored 450 marks out of 500, your calculation would be (450 ÷ 500) × 100 = 90%.

Why Calculate Your Percentage?

Knowing your percentage helps in:

  • Evaluating your academic performance
  • Setting goals for improvement
  • Understanding where you stand in class
  • Applying for scholarships or higher education

Grading System Reference

Our calculator uses a standard grading system:

  • 90% and above: A+ (Excellent)
  • 80-89%: A (Very Good)
  • 70-79%: B (Good)
  • 60-69%: C (Average)
  • 50-59%: D (Below Average)
  • Below 50%: F (Fail)
