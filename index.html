How to Calculate Marks Percentage

Calculating your marks percentage is a simple process that helps you understand your academic performance. The formula used is:

(Obtained Marks ÷ Total Marks) × 100 = Percentage

For example, if you scored 450 marks out of 500, your calculation would be (450 ÷ 500) × 100 = 90%.
