About Our Marks Percentage Calculator

Our Mission

At Marks Percentage Calculator, our mission is to provide students with simple, accurate tools to help them understand their academic performance. We believe that by making percentage calculations easy and accessible, we can help students focus more on their studies and less on complex calculations.

How It Works

Our calculator uses the standard percentage formula that's universally accepted in educational institutions. Simply enter your obtained marks and the total possible marks, and our system instantly calculates your percentage along with a corresponding grade.

Why Use Our Calculator?

  • Accuracy: Our calculations are precise and reliable
  • Speed: Get results instantly without manual calculations
  • Free: No cost, no registration required
  • Accessible: Works on all devices - phones, tablets, and computers

Our Team

We're a group of educators and developers who saw a need for simple academic tools. Our team combines teaching experience with technical expertise to create useful resources for students worldwide.

Future Plans

We're continuously working to add more educational tools to our platform, including GPA calculators, CGPA converters, and subject-specific performance analyzers.
